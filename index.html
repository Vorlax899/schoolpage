About the School

Shree Shanti Namuna Secondary School, located in Nepal, is committed to providing quality education and shaping disciplined, confident students. The school offers programs in Science, Management, Humanities, and Computer & ICT streams, supported by experienced teachers and modern facilities. Beyond academics, students are encouraged to participate in sports, arts, and cultural activities, fostering overall development and preparing them to contribute positively to society.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To create knowledgeable and disciplined students.

Mission: To provide modern and inclusive education.
